Diagnostics and Remote Access Interfaces

The following tags select the interfaces and transport protocols used by the netX Diagnostics and Remote Access component ("marshaller"). Interfaces are physical connections between the external client and the marshaller, transport protocols define the "language" used by the client and the marshaller.

Caution:

You must always enable at least one interface AND one transport protocol. Otherwise you lock yourself out of the device and will be unable to replace the firmware.
